About me


My digital journey started with coding Basic on the Apple 2.

In 1994, I was hired by a local BBS turned service provider to experiment with this thing called "The Internet."

While attending college, I was offered a position as an entry level HTML developer. Since then I've been learning and implimenting new languages and technologies as they emerge.

My interests aren't limited to a career progression. I also enjoy board / video games, movie making / VFX, composing music, tinkering with electronics, remote-control aircraft, model making, and many other project based activity.
